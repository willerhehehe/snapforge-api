Refund Policy

Last updated: April 15, 2026

1. Free Plan

The Free plan is provided at no cost. No refunds apply.

2. Paid Plans (Pro and Business)

We want you to be satisfied with SnapForge. If you're not happy with the Service:

  • Within 14 days of your first payment: You may request a full refund, no questions asked.
  • After 14 days: Refunds are handled on a case-by-case basis. Contact us to discuss.

3. How to Request a Refund

To request a refund, email us at willerhehehe@gmail.com with:

  • Your account email address
  • The reason for your refund request
  • Your payment date

We aim to process refund requests within 5 business days.

4. Cancellation

  • You may cancel your subscription at any time.
  • Cancellation takes effect at the end of your current billing period.
  • You will continue to have access to your paid plan features until the end of the billing period.
  • No partial refunds are provided for unused time within a billing period (except within the 14-day refund window).

5. Service Issues

If the Service experiences significant downtime or issues that prevent you from using it, we may offer:

  • Account credit for the affected period
  • Extension of your billing period
  • A pro-rated refund at our discretion

6. Refund Method

Refunds are processed through our payment partner (Paddle) and returned to the original payment method. Processing times may vary depending on your bank or payment provider.

7. Contact

For refund requests or billing questions, contact us at willerhehehe@gmail.com.
